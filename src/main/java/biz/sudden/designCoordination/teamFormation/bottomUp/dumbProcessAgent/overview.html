This folder contains an agent suitable for building simple demos like the demoManchester0606 and the Watertanl demo. These agents are essentially a skeleton of what a process agent might look like. They can fully participate in the required protocols, detect potential extensions and the like. However they are limited to: (1) having one & only one declared capability (which is moreover fixed rather than flexibly defined), (2) Agreeing to any and all extensions proposed to them. (3) Always proposing their declared capability when a possiblity presents itself. All of these represent substantial simplifications over the potential behaviour of agents within this system.
